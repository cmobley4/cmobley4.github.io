Mechanical Engineering Senior Design

The purpose of this project was to integrate an AUBO-i5 collaborative robot with both a gripper and camera vision system to perform an object recognition and manipulation demonstration for our sponsor Aubo Robotics. This demonstration was created alongside three other members of the University of Tennessee Mechanical Engineering program. The demonstration outline was to identify parts on a table based on unique Aruco markers, pick them up, and place them in a container. In addition, the AUBO-i5 robot is a collaborative system so an additional objective was to integrate a trip laser safety system for reduced speeds and safety zones within a designated work area.
